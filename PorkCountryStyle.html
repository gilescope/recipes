Pork Country Style

A great winter warmer.

Serves
Preparation: 30.0 minutes Start:
Cooking: 70.0 minutes End:

Ingredients
1.0 tsp oil
1 lb diced boneless pork
0.5 large sweed
2.0 carrots diced
2.0 parsnips sliced
2 leaks sliced
2.0 celery sticks diced
1.25 pints boiling beef stock
2.0 tbsp tomato pure
2.0 tbsp chopped parsley
1 to taste salt and pepper

Topping Ingredients
140 g plain flour
1 tspn backing powder
6 table spoon low fat fromage frais
3 table spoon chopped parsley

Method
Using the LARGE casorol dish
Preheat oven to GM4 (180 degrees for fan assisted)
Brown (seal) the pork in the caserol disk on the hob
Add veg and put in oven WITH LID ON
Stir in stock, tomarto puree and parsley.
Season with salt and pepper, COVER and cook for 1-1.25 hours.

Method for the topping
Sift the flour, backing powder and seasoning.
Stir in fromage frais and parsley and mix to a soft dough.
Roll to 1/2inch and cut into 12-15 triangles.
Remove the caserol disk from the oven and raise the temperature to GM7 (210 degrees c)
Arrange the triangles on top (overlapping is fine).
Bake WITHOUT THE LID on for 15-20 mins until golden.

Service: Serve hot.
